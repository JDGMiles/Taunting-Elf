Sound of Sadness

A page which progressively zooms an image to the Sound of Silence meme.

To set a custom image and settings use https://jdgmiles.github.io/Taunting-Elf/index.html? followed by the URL of the image you want to use, the x-coordinate of the focal point, the y-coordinate of the focal point, the zoom factor, then finally the frame rate.

For example: https://jdgmiles.github.io/Taunting-Elf/
index.html?http://cookiesbecrumbling.com/
